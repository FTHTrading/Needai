# SPOKEN VOICE PROMPTS FOR VANITY NUMBERS

This file contains natural language voice prompts for AI systems to read phone numbers aloud.

## Best Practices for Voice AI

**DO:**
- Say the area code as a group
- Say the vanity word naturally
- Add contextual information ("for storm damage", "for legal help")
- Use pauses between sections

**DON'T:**
- Spell out letters one by one
- Say "dash" or "hyphen"
- Rush through the number
- Mix digits and letters in confusing ways

## Advanced Voice Patterns

### For Callback Confirmations
"I'll have someone call you back at {their_number}. In the meantime, you can reach us directly at {vanity_number}."

### For Escalations
"I'm connecting you to a specialist now. If we get disconnected, please call back at {vanity_number}."

### For Follow-ups
"Your reference number is {ref_number}. Save this number along with our direct line: {vanity_number}."

### For Regional Routing
"For {region} residents, call {regional_vanity_number}. For all other areas, call {national_vanity_number}."
