# 📐 BILLBOARD PRINT SPECIFICATIONS

**Production-ready specs for billboard companies and print vendors**

---

## 🎨 STANDARD SIZES & SPECIFICATIONS

### **14' × 48' Bulletin (Most Common)**
- **Dimensions:** 168" × 576" (14' × 48')
- **Resolution:** 15 DPI minimum (billboard viewing distance)
- **Safe Zone:** 18" margin on all sides
- **Live Area:** 132" × 540"
- **File Format:** PDF, EPS, or TIFF
- **Color Mode:** CMYK
- **Bleed:** 3" all sides

### **10' × 20' Poster**
- **Dimensions:** 120" × 240" (10' × 20')
- **Resolution:** 15 DPI minimum
- **Safe Zone:** 12" margin on all sides
- **Live Area:** 96" × 216"
- **File Format:** PDF, EPS, or TIFF
- **Color Mode:** CMYK
- **Bleed:** 2" all sides

### **Digital Billboard**
- **Dimensions:** 1920 × 1080 pixels (HD) or 1080 × 1920 (vertical)
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI
- **File Format:** PNG, JPG, or MP4 (animated)
- **Color Mode:** RGB
- **Duration:** 8 seconds per rotation (standard)
- **File Size:** Under 1MB for static, under 5MB for video

---

## 📏 LAYOUT RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### **Primary Word**
- **Size:** 70% of billboard height minimum
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif, maximum weight
- **Tracking:** Tight (eliminate letter gaps)
- **Alignment:** Center
- **Position:** Upper 60% of board

### **Phone Number**
- **Size:** 20% of billboard height minimum
- **Font:** Same as primary word, bold
- **Tracking:** Normal (readable digits)
- **Alignment:** Center
- **Position:** Lower 40% of board

### **Safe Zone**
- **Keep ALL text inside safe zone**
- No text within 18" of edge (14×48)
- No text within 12" of edge (10×20)
- Prevents obstruction by frame/lighting

## 🎨 COLOR PALETTES (DAY/NIGHT OPTIMIZED)

### **HIGH CONTRAST DAY (Recommended)**
- **Background:** #000000 (Pure Black)
- **Text:** #FFFFFF (Pure White)
- **Contrast Ratio:** 21:1 (AAA+)

### **HIGH CONTRAST NIGHT (Recommended)**
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (Pure White)
- **Text:** #000000 (Pure Black)
- **Contrast Ratio:** 21:1 (AAA+)

### **STORM (Blue Variant)**
- **Background:** #001F3F (Dark Blue)
- **Text:** #FFDC00 (High-Vis Yellow)
- **Contrast Ratio:** 12:1 (AAA)

### **HAIL (Emergency Variant)**
- **Background:** #C41E3A (Red)
- **Text:** #FFFFFF (White)
- **Contrast Ratio:** 8:1 (AA)

### **LAW (Professional Variant)**
- **Background:** #1A1A1A (Near Black)
- **Text:** #FFD700 (Gold)
- **Contrast Ratio:** 10:1 (AAA)
