### **Qualified Lead Criteria:**
- ✅ Valid address in your service area
- ✅ Damage confirmed (not just inquiring)
- ✅ Contact information provided
- ✅ Caller is decision-maker or authorized to contract

### **Lead Tiers:**
1. **Hot Lead** - Active damage, immediate need, ready to hire

3. **Cold Lead** - Older damage, price shopping, no urgency

### **Quality Filters:**
