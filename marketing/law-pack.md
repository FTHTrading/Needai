## 🤖 AI CALL FLOW

**Caller dials: 888-LAW-AI (or any LAW number)**

### **AI Greeting:**
```
"Thank you for calling. I'm an AI legal assistant 
here to help. What type of legal matter do you 
need assistance with?"
```

### **Qualification Questions:**

**1. Practice Area:**
- Personal injury
- Criminal defense
- Family law
- Immigration
- Employment
- Real estate
- Other

**2. Details (based on practice area):**

**If Personal Injury:**
- What happened?
- When did it happen?
- Were you injured? How badly?
- Did you seek medical treatment?
- Other party information?

**If Criminal:**
- What are the charges?
- Have you been arrested?
- When is your court date?
- Do you currently have a lawyer?

**If Family Law:**
- What's the issue? (divorce, custody, support, etc.)
- Are you currently married/separated?
- Any children involved?
- Any current court orders?

**3. Contact Information:**
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email
- Best time to call

**4. Urgency:**
- Immediate (arrest, court tomorrow)
- Urgent (within days)
- Standard (within weeks)

### **AI Confirmation:**
```
"I understand you need help with [practice area] 
regarding [issue]. An attorney will review your 
case and contact you at [phone] within [timeframe]. 
Is there anything else I should know?"
```

### **Lead Delivery:**
- Immediate email to attorney/firm
- SMS alert (optional)
- CRM integration
- Lead includes: practice area, details, urgency, contact
