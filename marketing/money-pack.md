## 🤖 AI CALL FLOW

**Caller dials: 888-CASH-AI (or 888-BUCK-AI, 866-BANK-AI)**

### **AI Greeting:**
```
"Thank you for calling. I'm here to help with 
your financial needs. What type of assistance 
are you looking for today?"
```

### **Qualification Questions:**
1. **Need type:** Cash advance, loan, banking, bill help, other
2. **Amount:** How much do you need?
3. **Urgency:** When do you need it?
4. **Employment:** Are you currently employed?
5. **Credit:** Credit rating (if known)
6. **Contact:** Phone, email, best time to call

### **AI Confirmation:**
```
"I've got your information. You're looking for 
[amount] in [timeframe] for [purpose]. A financial 
specialist will contact you within the next 
2 hours at [phone number]. Is that correct?"
```

### **Lead Delivery:**
- Immediate email notification
- SMS alert (optional)
- CRM integration
- Lead includes: amount, urgency, employment, contact
