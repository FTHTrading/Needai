## 🎯 CAMPAIGN CONCEPT

**Pure NEED numbers capture ALL intent.**

Instead of separate billboards for money, law, storm, HVAC, etc., these billboards say:

```
NEED HELP?
CALL 844-NEED
```

**What happens:**
1. Caller dials `844-669-6333` (TRUE NEED number)
2. AI answers: "What do you need help with today?"
3. Caller describes their need
4. AI routes to: money, law, storm, claims, HVAC, or general assistance

**This is MORE POWERFUL** because it captures every type of need with one billboard.

## 🤖 AI CALL FLOW (UNIVERSAL INTAKE)

### **Call arrives at 844-NEED or 912-NEED:**

**AI greeting:**
```
"Thank you for calling. I'm here to help. 
What do you need assistance with today?"
```

**Caller responds with their need. AI identifies category:**

---

### **Scenario 1: Money/Financial**
**Caller:** "I need money" / "I need a loan" / "I need help with bills"

**AI:**
```
"I understand you need financial assistance. 
I can connect you with options for emergency cash, 
loans, or financial services. Let me get a few 
details..."
```

**Routes to:** MONEY persona (888-CASH-AI equivalent)

---

### **Scenario 2: Legal**
**Caller:** "I need a lawyer" / "I need legal help" / "I'm in legal trouble"

**AI:**
```
"I understand you need legal assistance. I can 
help connect you with legal services. What type 
of legal matter is this regarding?"
```

**Routes to:** LAW persona (888-LAW-AI equivalent)

---

### **Scenario 3: Storm/Property Damage**
**Caller:** "My roof is damaged" / "I need storm damage help" / "Tree fell on my house"

**AI:**
```
"I understand you have property damage. Let me 
get your information so we can help right away. 
What's your address?"
```

**Routes to:** STORM persona (470-STORM equivalent)

---

### **Scenario 4: HVAC Emergency**
**Caller:** "My heat isn't working" / "AC is broken" / "No heating"

**AI:**
```
"I understand you have an HVAC emergency. Let me 
get your details so we can send help. What's 
your address and what's not working?"
```

**Routes to:** HVAC persona (833-HVAC-NOW equivalent)

---

### **Scenario 5: Insurance Claims**
**Caller:** "I need to file a claim" / "I need help with my insurance claim"

**AI:**
```
"I can help you with your insurance claim. Let 
me collect some information. Who is your 
insurance carrier?"
```

**Routes to:** CLAIMS persona (844-CLAIM equivalent)

---

### **Scenario 6: General/Unclear**
**Caller:** "I don't know" / "I need help with everything" / unclear

**AI:**
```
"I understand you're going through a difficult 
time. Let me ask a few questions to see how I 
can best help you. Can you tell me what's 
happening?"
```

**Routes to:** NEED persona (general assistance, further triage)

## ✅ ADVANTAGES OF NEED CAMPAIGN

1. **Captures ALL intent** - One billboard = all verticals
2. **Lower barrier** - Caller doesn't need to know category
3. **Crisis-friendly** - People in crisis often don't know what they need
4. **Flexible routing** - Backend handles categorization
5. **Brand consolidation** - One memorable number
6. **Efficient deployment** - Fewer billboard designs needed
7. **Geographic scaling** - Easy to add markets

---

## ⚠️ CONSIDERATIONS

1. **Longer AI qualification** - AI must determine need before routing
2. **Lead diversity** - Mix of verticals (some low-value, some high-value)
3. **Routing complexity** - Backend must handle multi-vertical routing
4. **Partner coordination** - If reselling leads, need partners in all verticals
5. **Conversion tracking** - Harder to attribute by original intent
