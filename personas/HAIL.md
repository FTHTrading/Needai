```
"To properly assess hail damage, we need to document:

* Hail stone size (if known)
* Duration of the storm
* Wind speed during hail
* Photos of damage patterns
* Location on roof/vehicle
* Age of roof/shingles

This helps determine if it's storm-related damage."
```
